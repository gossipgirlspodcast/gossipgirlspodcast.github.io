About Us

Since meeting in 2014, C and S's texts and sexts were constantly littered with references to Gossip Girl. In 2016, they both had the nearly simultaneous idea to launch a podcast about the show (which had ended three years prior). Gossip Girls is a podcast hosted by two women of color, giving a unique take on some Rich, White teens living on the Upper East Side. None of this is relatable except fot the fact that the characters are fucked up, just like us! Follow us on Twitter @chermusings and @kalehummus
